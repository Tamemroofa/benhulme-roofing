Roof Replacement

Here is what you need to know.

When a roof becomes too old or too weak due to previous incorrect installation, it goes beyond repair where we may need to strip the roof to replace the material under the main roof.

  • safely remove old material.
  • Dispose old matertial and install fresh ones.

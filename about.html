Our Story

Our roofing fairies have come to the conclusion that your roof is the most important part of your home. and as my years went by as an apprentice, I realized that roofs were not getting the respect they deserved. your average Joe shmo who knows nothing about roofing, whipping up a replacement quote.

    One day I came home and I asked myself, "When does it end?" Cowboys stormed the trade due to the shortage of roofers, and something needed to be done, so after I finished my roofing apprenticeship, I got my cousin Dan, who also did his apprenticeship in flat roofing. and we started our company.
    It wasn't long till we became the avengers of roofing, dealing with quality, honesty, and precision.
